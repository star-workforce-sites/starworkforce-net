Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Our Commitment

StarTekk is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards.

Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen readers
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Alternative text for images
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Responsive design for various devices and zoom levels
  • Clear heading structure and landmarks
  • Form labels and error messages

Known Limitations

Despite our efforts, some pages may not yet be fully accessible. We are working to address these issues:

  • Some third-party embedded content may not be fully accessible
  • PDF documents are being reviewed for accessibility
  • Some complex interactive elements are being improved

Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to work with:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Screen magnification software
  • Browser zoom features

Third-Party Content

Some content on our website is provided by third parties (Google Analytics, embedded videos, etc.). We cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content but select vendors with strong accessibility practices.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly:

  • Audit our website for accessibility issues
  • Train our team on accessibility best practices
  • Update our code and content based on feedback
  • Test with assistive technologies
