Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Our Commitment

Startekk LLC and STAR Workforce Solutions are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

Our websites conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. We use the following methods to ensure accessibility:

  • Semantic HTML for screen reader compatibility
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Alternative text for images
  • Clear and consistent navigation
  • Mobile-responsive design
