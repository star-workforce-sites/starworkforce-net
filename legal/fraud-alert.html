Fraud Alert

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

⚠️ Important Security Notice

Be aware of fraudulent communications claiming to be from Startekk or STAR Workforce. We will never ask for sensitive information via email or text.

Common Fraud Schemes

  • Fake job postings requesting payment for applications
  • Phishing emails requesting login credentials
  • Impersonation of our staff on social media
  • Requests for personal banking information
  • Work-from-home scams using our company name

How to Verify Communication

Legitimate communications from us will:

  • Come from @startekk.net or @starworkforce.net email addresses
  • Never request payment for job applications
  • Never ask for sensitive financial information via email
  • Include direct contact information for verification
