Common Scam Tactics

Scammers may:

  • Offer jobs without interviews: Legitimate employers always conduct interviews
  • Request payment: We NEVER ask for money for job applications, training, or equipment
  • Use personal email accounts: Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook (we only use @startekk.net or @starworkforce.net)
  • Promise unrealistic salaries: Too-good-to-be-true offers are often scams
  • Request sensitive information upfront: SSN, bank details before hiring
  • Conduct interviews via text/chat only: We use phone or video calls
  • Send unsolicited job offers: We don't send job offers to people who haven't applied

What We NEVER Do

StarTekk and Star Workforce Will NEVER:

  • Ask you to pay for a job opportunity
  • Request payment for training, equipment, or background checks
  • Ask for your bank account, credit card, or SSN before a formal offer
  • Conduct entire hiring process via text message or instant messaging
  • Send checks and ask you to deposit and forward funds
  • Offer jobs without proper interviews and screening
  • Use Gmail, Yahoo, or personal email accounts
