Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to both StarTekk, LLC and its division Star Workforce Solutions (collectively "StarTekk," "we," "us," or "our").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title
  • Professional Information: Resume, work history, skills, certifications, references
  • Account Information: Username, password (encrypted), profile preferences
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support requests, consultation notes
  • Payment Information: Billing address (payment processing handled by third-party processors)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, search queries
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, analytics cookies, preference cookies
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Provide staffing, consulting, and technology solutions
  • Matching & Placement: Match candidates with job opportunities and clients with talent
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries, send updates, provide customer support
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns, improve our services, develop new features
  • Marketing: Send newsletters, promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations, enforce terms, protect rights
  • Security: Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Share Information With:

  • Clients: With your consent, we share candidate information with potential employers
  • Candidates: Job opportunities matching your profile
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Vercel), email (third-party), analytics (Google Analytics), payment processors
  • Business Partners: Offshore teams, partner staffing agencies (with appropriate agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement, regulatory bodies, courts when required by law

4.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • SOC 2 Type II: Compliance for certain services (Star AI products)
  • Incident Response: Procedures for data breach notification

Note: No system is 100% secure. While we implement industry-standard security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies. You can manage cookie preferences through our Cookie Settings tool. See our separate Cookie Policy for details.

Types of Cookies:

  • Essential: Required for site functionality (cannot be disabled)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage statistics (can be disabled)
  • Functional: Remember preferences and settings (can be disabled)
  • Marketing: Track conversions and campaigns (can be disabled)

7. Your Privacy Rights

  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information

8. Data Retention

  • Candidate Data: Retained for 3 years after last activity or until deletion requested

  • Legal Requirements: Some data must be retained for tax, employment, or legal compliance

9. International Data Transfers

We operate globally with offices in the USA, India, Canada, and Australia. Your data may be transferred to and processed in these countries. We use standard contractual clauses and other safeguards to ensure adequate protection.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.
