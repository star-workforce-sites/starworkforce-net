Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve functionality, and provide analytics about how the site is used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. You cannot disable them.

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security cookies: Protect against fraud and ensure secure connections
  • Preference cookies: Remember your cookie consent choices

2.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website.

  • Google Analytics: Tracks page views, session duration, bounce rates
  • Performance monitoring: Identifies technical issues and load times

You can disable analytics cookies through our Cookie Settings.

2.3 Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies remember your preferences to enhance your experience.

  • Language preferences: Remember your preferred language
  • Theme settings: Remember light/dark mode choices (if applicable)
  • Recently viewed: Show recently viewed jobs or services

2.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional)

These cookies track your activity to show relevant advertisements.

  • Conversion tracking: Measure effectiveness of marketing campaigns
  • Retargeting: Show relevant ads on other websites

Currently not in use, but may be implemented in the future.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Web analytics (privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Vercel: Web hosting and performance (privacy policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

4. Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

Deleted when you close your browser (temporary).

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a set period:

  • Cookie consent: 12 months
  • Google Analytics: Up to 24 months
  • Preferences: 6-12 months

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner with options to:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies
  • Decline Optional: Only essential cookies
  • Manage Preferences: Choose specific cookie types

5.2 Cookie Settings Tool

You can change your preferences anytime using our Cookie Settings tool (link in footer).
