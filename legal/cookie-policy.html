Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our websites. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our sites:

  • StartTekk.net: G-8BFZG3R9ZR
  • StarWorkforce.net: G-W3QGRJGB6M

These cookies collect anonymized data about page visits, time spent, and navigation patterns.

2.3 Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences like language settings and cookie consent choices.

2.4 Marketing Cookies

Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Understand website usage and improve user experience
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze traffic patterns and popular content
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

4. Your Cookie Choices

You control your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner when you first visit our website. You can:

  • Accept all cookies: Allow all cookies for full website functionality
  • Decline cookies: Only essential cookies will be used
  • Manage preferences: Choose which types of cookies to allow

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. Third parties include:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and reporting
  • Social Media Platforms: Social sharing buttons
  • Content Delivery Networks: Faster content delivery

6. Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different retention periods:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (typically 1-2 years)
  • Cookie consent: Your preferences are stored for 12 months
