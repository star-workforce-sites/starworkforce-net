Do Not Sell My Personal Information

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request that we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Important Notice

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties for monetary consideration.

What This Means

While we do not sell personal information, we do share data with service providers for business purposes as described in our Privacy Policy. This includes analytics providers, payment processors, and hosting services.

Your CCPA Rights

  • Right to Know: Request information about data we collect and share
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (though we don't sell)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of exercising rights

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us:

Email: privacy@startekk.net

We will respond within 45 days of receiving your verifiable request.
