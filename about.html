Our Story

Founded over two decades ago, STAR Workforce Services has grown from a regional IT staffing firm to a global provider of technology talent solutions. Our journey has been driven by one simple principle: connecting exceptional technology professionals with companies that need them.

Today, we serve Fortune 500 companies, innovative startups, and everything in between. With offices spanning four continents, we've successfully placed over 10,000 IT professionals in roles that advance both their careers and their employers' missions.
