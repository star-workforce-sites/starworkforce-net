Our Story

Founded over two decades ago, STAR Workforce Services (a division of Startekk, LLC) has grown from a regional IT staffing firm into a global provider of technology and clinical healthcare talent solutions.

In 2019, we expanded into healthcare clinical staffing — recognizing the growing need for credentialed clinical professionals at hospitals, health systems, and correctional healthcare facilities across the USA. Today our Healthcare Division places Travel RNs, Allied Health specialists, Behavioral Health clinicians, and Healthcare IT professionals nationwide.

Our dual capability — IT staffing and healthcare clinical staffing under one roof — simplifies vendor relationships and gives our clients a single trusted partner for all workforce needs.
