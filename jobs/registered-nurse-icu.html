Registered Nurse — ICU (Travel)

HWL Health System Utica, NY Travel Contract · 13 weeks Competitive
Apply Now →

Job Overview

Experienced ICU Travel RN needed for a high-acuity 42-bed critical care unit at HWL Health System in Utica, NY. You will provide specialized nursing care to critically ill patients requiring advanced monitoring and life-support interventions across Neuro/Medical, Cardiothoracic, and Surgical/Trauma ICU settings. Night shift, 12-hour blocks, 36 hours/week.

Schedule: Night Shift 6:30pm–7am, 3 days/week, 36 hours/week

Responsibilities

  • Provide direct nursing care to critically ill patients across Neuro/Medical, Cardiothoracic, and Surgical/Trauma ICU
  • Monitor and manage patients on ventilators, IABP, CRRT, ventriculostomy, ICP monitoring, and targeted temperature management
  • Administer and titrate critical care medications and infusions
  • Collaborate with physicians and interdisciplinary teams to develop and implement patient care plans
  • Maintain accurate clinical documentation in EPIC EMR
  • Float to other units as required (excluding Behavioral Health and ED unless holding patients)

Requirements & Qualifications

  • Minimum 2 years of ICU experience required (ICU, SICU, CTICU, Trauma ICU, Medical ICU, or Neuro ICU)
  • CRRT experience required (Prismaflex, PRISMA, or NxStage)
  • Active New York State RN license verified through NURSYS
  • Prior travel nursing experience required
  • EPIC EMR experience required
  • BLS — American Heart Association required
  • ACLS — American Heart Association required
  • NIH Stroke Scale (NIHSS) required
  • Cardiac and heart surgery experience highly preferred
  • Local candidates within 75 miles considered at local rate

Education

Associate Degree in Nursing (ADN) or Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN). Scrub color: Navy.

Skills & Certifications

ICU Critical Care CRRT BLS ACLS NIHSS EPIC EMR Travel RN Ventilator Mgmt
